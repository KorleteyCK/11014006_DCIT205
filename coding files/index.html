WELCOME


Welcome to The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana, The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana stands out as one of Africa's largest and most esteemed institutions in the field. Offering an extensive array of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, along with short courses and opportunities for professional development, the department is unwavering in its dedication to furnishing students with a solid foundation in computer science and the requisite skills to thrive in the constantly evolving realm of technology.Among the undergraduate programs are the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1), as well as the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. The postgraduate offerings encompass the Master of Science in Computer Science, the Master of Science in Information Technology, and the Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science. Distinguished by a robust research program, the department hosts a cadre of globally recognized researchers with interests spanning artificial intelligence, computer networks, data mining, information security, and software engineering. A focal point of the department's mission is delivering a high-quality education, facilitated by a faculty composed of field experts who are devoted to teaching and mentoring. State-of-the-art facilities, including a computer lab, network lab, and robotics lab, augment the learning experience. Graduates from the department are highly sought after by employers worldwide, finding placement in diverse sectors such as technology, finance, and healthcare.The Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana is a dynamic and stimulating environment for studying computer science. The department remains steadfast in its commitment to equipping students with the skills and knowledge essential for success in the ever-changing landscape of technology.

Library


The University of Ghana has a comprehensive library system that serves the needs of its students, faculty, and staff. The system consists of the Balme Library, which is the main library, as well as a number of smaller branch libraries located throughout the campus.The Balme Library is the largest library in Ghana and one of the largest in Africa. It houses a collection of over one million books, journals, and other materials. The library also has a wide range of electronic resources, including online databases, e-books, and e-journals. The Balme Library is open to the public and offers a variety of services, including reference and research assistance, interlibrary loan, and document delivery.
