Libraries


The University of Ghana has a comprehensive library system that serves the needs of its students, faculty, and staff. The system consists of the Balme Library, which is the main library, as well as a number of smaller branch libraries located throughout the campus.The Balme Library is the largest library in Ghana and one of the largest in Africa. It houses a collection of over one million books, journals, and other materials. The library also has a wide range of electronic resources, including online databases, e-books, and e-journals. The Balme Library is open to the public and offers a variety of services, including reference and research assistance, interlibrary loan, and document delivery. Branch Libraries in addition to the Balme Library, the University of Ghana also has a number of smaller branch libraries. These branch libraries are located in the various colleges, schools, institutes, and departments on campus. The branch libraries house collections of materials that are relevant to the specific needs of the academic units they serve. The branch libraries offer a variety of services, including reference and research assistance, interlibrary loan, and document delivery. Special Collections; The University of Ghana Library System also houses a number of special collections. These special collections include rare books, manuscripts, archives, and other materials. The special collections are open to researchers and are a valuable resource for scholars who are studying the history of Ghana and the University of Ghana.The University of Ghana Library System is committed to providing its users with access to the information they need to succeed. The library system is constantly expanding its collections and services to meet the needs of its users.
